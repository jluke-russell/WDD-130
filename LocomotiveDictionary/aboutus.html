About Us

Here at Locomotive Dictionary pride ourselves with helping the novice railfan learn about CSX locomotive power. Our experience in the field has been invaluble to our company and will provide a good starting place for anyone.

I first came up with the idea for Locomotive Dictionary when I began railfanning. I have always loved trains, and watching them. However, I did not know how to tell them apart, thus I searched for resources to help me, but I did not find anything comprehensive and simple enough for the novice railfan. Locomotive dictionary changes that.

We hope that Locomotive Dictionary will help you learn more about diesel-electric locomotives. We also hope that you can use the description of each locomotive to positively identify them on your next railfan adventure!
