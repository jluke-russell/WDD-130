Overview

Purpose

FreightTrain.com will help you learn everything you need to know about modern American locomotive power and freight rail operations. Our web based locomotive dictionary is complete with photos, specifications, stories, and videos; there is no other source that has such a comprehensive and inclusive guide to modern American locomotive power and freight rail operations. At FreightTrain.com you will learn how the modern diesel-electric locomotives work and how railroads work.

Audience

People who want to learn more about CSX trains.
